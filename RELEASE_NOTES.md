## ✨ Key Features

### 🏠 Home Tab
- **Latest News Feed:** Browse the most recent news articles with auto-refreshing content
- **Featured News Slider:** Automatic slideshow of top 5 featured articles with 5-second intervals
- **Real-time Updates:** Pull-to-refresh functionality for the latest content
- **Time Stamps:** Smart time display showing "X hours/days ago" format
- **Quick Actions:** Add articles to favorites directly from the home feed

### 📂 Categories Tab
- **News Categories:** Browse news by different categories
- **Category-specific Content:** Filter articles by selected category
- **Dynamic Loading:** Seamless category switching with loading indicators
- **Organized Content:** Clean category-based navigation

### 📺 Live TV Tab
- **Live Streaming:** Watch live TV content directly in the app
- **Multiple Video Sources:** Support for YouTube, iframe, and direct video URLs
- **Auto-play Support:** Automatic video playback with mute option
- **Error Handling:** Graceful fallback when video content is unavailable

### ⭐ Favorites System
- **Save Articles:** Add news articles to your personal favorites list
- **Quick Access:** Dedicated favorites tab for saved content
- **Persistent Storage:** Favorites are saved locally on your device
- **Toast Notifications:** Confirmation messages when adding to favorites

- Android 7.0 (API level 24) or higher

1. Download the APK file
2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" if needed
3. Install the application
4. Launch and enjoy the news experience
